Identified Symptoms

  • Water-soaked, dark green or black lesions starting at leaf tips.

Pathogen Description

Late blight is caused by the destructive water mold Phytophthora infestans. It targets all above-ground crop sections causing massive plant rot inside days.

Clinical Treatment Plan

Immediately prune and destroy infected plant debris. Treat with liquid copper protectant fungicides early, and avoid over-watering of leaf structures.
